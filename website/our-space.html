Our Space

Lounge, Patio & Courtyard

Three distinct vibes under one roof — upscale lounge inside, air-conditioned covered patio, and an open-air courtyard oasis near Grandscape.

Inside

Upscale Lounge

Comfortable lounge seating, premium hookah, signature cocktails, and wide-screen TVs throughout — whether you're here for the game or a night out with friends, the lounge is the heart of Twisted.

175″ LED wall, 150″ screen, and large TVs in every corner paired with a hi-fi audio system for game day and live events.

Covered Patio

Air-Conditioned Covered Patio

Stay cool year-round on our covered patio — brunch, dinner, or drinks with the Texas sky above and climate-controlled comfort below.

Perfect for groups who want the outdoor feel without sacrificing comfort, any season of the year.

Courtyard

Open-Air Courtyard Oasis

Hookah and cocktails under the trees in our open-air courtyard — relaxed resort vibes right in The Colony.

Our guests' favorite spot to unwind: lively energy, great music, and an atmosphere that feels a world away from the everyday.

Located at Cascades Court near Grandscape and Topgolf — the go-to spot for North Dallas including Frisco, Lewisville, Plano, and Carrollton.
